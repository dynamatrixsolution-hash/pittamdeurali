## Local SEO Best Practices Checklist for Pitam Deurali

To maximize organic search and Google Maps visibility, ensure the following steps are taken in parallel with these meta tag updates:

1. **Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization:**
   * Claim and verify the GBP profile under the name: `Pitam Deurali Guest House`.
   * Set primary category to `Guest House` or `Lodge` and secondary categories to `Hotel` and `Nepalese Restaurant`.
   * Use the exact address in Pothana, Deurali, Dhampus, Kaski, Gandaki Province, Nepal.
   * Add high-resolution photos of rooms, the restaurant, and the Annapurna views from the property.

2. **Schema Markup Integration:**
   * Embed `Hotel` or `LodgingBusiness` JSON-LD schema on the Home page.
   * Link the website to the Google Business Profile coordinates using the `hasMap` schema property.

3. **Geographic Keyword Integration (NAP consistency):**
   * Keep Name, Address, and Phone number (NAP) identical across the website footer, booking forms, and external directory listings (TripAdvisor, Booking.com, etc.).
